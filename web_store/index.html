Welcome to Chester House Crafts

About Us

At Chester House Crafts, our work is inspired by the natural beauty of Eyemouth and the rolling landscapes of the Scottish Borders.
Based in Chester House, an 18th-century townhouse that’s now both our family home and creative studio, we design and make unique crafts that celebrate nature, heritage, and imagination.
From traditional skills to fresh ideas, everything we create is handmade with care and rooted in a love of place. Whether you’re looking for something special for your home, a thoughtful gift, or simply a piece of the Borders to carry with you, you’ll find it here.

About Us

Our crafts are inspired by the beautiful natural surroundings of Eyemouth, in the Scottish Borders.

Chester House is an elegant 18th-century townhouse, originally built by the owners of Chesterbank Farm as their home away from the farm. Today, it serves as both our family home and the creative heart of Chester House Crafts — where traditional artistry and contemporary design come together in our art and craft studios.

Our Values

  • Craftsmanship — We believe in skill, care, and respect for materials.
  • Sustainability — Where possible, we use local and natural resources.
  • Community — We share our knowledge and welcome visitors into our creative world.
  • Heritage — We honour traditional methods while reimagining them for today.
